Ping Pong Game

Enter your name

Rules:
  1. The maximum name is 8 characters
  2. The maximum minutes is 100 minutes
  1. Player 1 on the left player 2 on the right
  2. To control player 1 press W for up S for down
  3. To control player 2, press Arrow Up for up, Arrow Down for down
  4. Press R if the ball is almost close to vertical, cooldown for 5 seconds
  5. The first ball will appear randomly
  6. The more scores your pong size will decrease
  7. The game will end based on the minute time you input
  8. You can save on the exit button and when game over
